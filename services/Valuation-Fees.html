Standard Guidelines for Billing of Multiple Assets

When 1+ properties of similar nature are considered for valuation, the values of these properties are clubbed to compute the fees and discount in the Professional Fee. The above-mentioned references provide details of which properties should be considered under this criteria for Bill as per the rule. According to which, properties of similar nature are considered as one for the billing purpose, while we consider the discount on the applied bill.

Reference of Rules regarding the Professional Fees

  1. WEALTH-TAX (FIRST AMENDMENT) RULES, 2009 Substitution of Rule 8C
  2. HANDBOOK ON POLICY, STANDARDS AND PROCEDURES FOR REAL ESTATE VALUATION BY BANKS AND HOUSING FINANCING SOCIETIES IN INDIA (Clause 1.2d, 1.6)
  3. Market Competition
